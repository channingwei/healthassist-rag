GENERAL HEALTH GUIDELINES

1. NUTRITION AND DIET
- Eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins
- Stay hydrated by drinking at least 8 glasses of water daily
- Limit processed foods, added sugars, and excessive salt
- Include healthy fats like omega-3 fatty acids from fish and nuts

2. PHYSICAL ACTIVITY
- Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity per week
- Include strength training exercises 2-3 times per week
- Take regular breaks from sitting, especially if working at a desk
- Find activities you enjoy to maintain consistency

3. SLEEP HYGIENE
- Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night
- Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
- Create a relaxing bedtime routine
- Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet
- Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime

4. MENTAL HEALTH
- Practice stress management techniques like meditation or deep breathing
- Maintain social connections and relationships
- Seek professional help if experiencing persistent negative emotions
- Take regular breaks and practice self-care

5. PREVENTIVE CARE
- Schedule regular check-ups with your healthcare provider
- Stay up to date with recommended vaccinations
- Get age-appropriate screenings (blood pressure, cholesterol, etc.)
- Practice good hygiene habits

6. SUBSTANCE USE
- Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke
- Limit alcohol consumption to moderate levels
- Avoid recreational drug use
- Be cautious with prescription medications

7. ENVIRONMENTAL HEALTH
- Maintain good indoor air quality
- Use sunscreen and protect against UV radiation
- Practice safe food handling and preparation
- Be aware of environmental hazards in your area

Remember: These are general guidelines. Always consult with healthcare professionals for personalized medical advice.
